What is Bombora & how does it work?

Bombora is the leading B2B intent data cooperative. It aggregates anonymous behavioural data from a network of 5,000+ premium B2B websites (think: industry publications, tech review sites, event forums). When employees at a target company consume content about a specific topic — say "event management" or "brand activation" — Bombora records this and calculates a surge score compared to that company's normal baseline.

The key insight: companies don't buy alone. Multiple employees research before a purchase. When you see a company with a surge score of 75+ on "experiential marketing", it means their team has been reading about it intensively — that's a buying signal.

Step-by-Step: Build the Engine

1

Set Up Bombora (or Apollo.io as Free Alternative)

Access Bombora directly at bombora.com or through HubSpot / Salesforce integrations. For a free start, use Apollo.io's intent feature which draws from a similar data co-op at no cost. Upload your Target Account List (200–500 company domains) and configure your intent topics.

  • Go to bombora.com → request demo → mention you're tracking "experiential marketing" and "B2B events" topics in India
  • Alternative free start: apollo.io → Signals tab → Intent → Select your topics → upload domain list
  • Set up Surge™ topic alerts for: Experiential Marketing, Brand Activation, Event Management, Corporate Events, Marketing Agency Search
  • Configure weekly digest email so intent data lands in your inbox every Monday morning
2

Build & Upload Your Target Account List (TAL)

Bombora is only valuable if you point it at the right companies. Build a list of 200–500 Indian companies that match Younion's ICP: FMCG, BFSI, Pharma, Tech companies with marketing teams large enough to run brand campaigns.

  • Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator → filter by: Company Size 500+, Industry (FMCG/Pharma/BFSI/Tech), Location India → export 300 companies
  • Export as CSV with columns: Company Name, Domain, Industry, City, Employee Count
  • Upload domain list to Bombora under "My Accounts" — this creates your watched account universe
  • Also upload to HubSpot CRM as a "Target Account" company list so intent data has a home to land in
3

Connect Bombora → CRM (HubSpot/Zoho/Salesforce)

Intent signals are useless if your sales team has to log into Bombora to check them. Use native integrations or Zapier to automatically push signals into your CRM as soon as they're detected.

  • HubSpot Integration: HubSpot App Marketplace → search "Bombora" → install → authenticate → map fields: Surge Score → Custom Property, Intent Topics → Notes
  • Create 3 custom CRM properties: "Bombora Surge Score" (number), "Intent Topics" (multi-select), "Intent Level" (dropdown: High/Medium/Low)
  • Build a CRM workflow: IF Surge Score ≥ 60 → Set Intent Level = High → Create Task for SDR → Send Slack notification
  • Zapier alternative: Bombora webhook → Zapier → HubSpot/Zoho CRM contact update (no code required)
4

Define Scoring Rules & Automate Actions

Not every signal deserves equal attention. Score and route signals so your SDR knows exactly who to call today versus who goes into nurture.

  • High Intent (Score 70–100): SDR calls within 24 hours → Personal email from founder/senior team → Add to LinkedIn outreach sequence
  • Medium Intent (Score 40–69): Add to email nurture sequence → LinkedIn connection request + message → Retargeting ad shown
  • Low Intent (Score 20–39): Add to newsletter → LinkedIn follow → No direct outreach yet
  • Set up Slack/WhatsApp alert: Daily 9am message: "🔥 3 new high-intent accounts from Bombora" with company name, score, and CRM link
5

Write Intent-Based Outreach (Using the Signal)

The biggest mistake SDRs make: getting a Bombora signal and sending a generic pitch. The signal gives you context — use it in your outreach to 3x your reply rate.

  • Don't say: "Hi, I'm from Younion, we do events and brand experiences..."
  • Do say: "Hi [Name], I noticed [Company] has been exploring experiential marketing options recently — we've helped similar FMCG brands like [X] create [specific result]. Would it make sense to connect?"
  • Build 3 email templates per intent level in HubSpot Sequences — reference the intent topic without revealing that you have their data
  • For High Intent accounts, personalise every email with the company's recent news/campaigns before sending

🛠️ 3rd Party Intent Tools for Younion

  • Bombora — Industry standard, 5000+ sites. Best for India enterprise accounts. Paid ~$1K/mo
  • Apollo.io Intent — Free tier available, overlaps with Bombora co-op. Start here. Free to start
  • G2 Buyer Intent — Shows who viewed your G2 profile/compared competitors. Free + Paid
  • ZoomInfo Intent — Strong for US/global accounts with India coverage growing. Paid
  • Clearbit Reveal — Identifies companies visiting younion.live via reverse IP. Freemium
  • TechTarget Priority Engine — Good for tech sector targets. Paid

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Buying intent data without a Target Account List — you'll drown in irrelevant signals
  • Not connecting signals to CRM — SDR misses alerts, leads go cold
  • Revealing you have intent data in outreach ("I see you've been researching...") — feels creepy, kills trust
  • Ignoring signal age — a 60-day-old signal is near worthless, act within 7 days
  • Using only one intent topic — track 5–8 topics to catch all buyer journey stages
  • No score decay rule — accounts that went cold should drop out of high-intent view automatically
  • Sales and marketing working from different data — maintain one CRM as single source of truth

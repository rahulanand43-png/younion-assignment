HOW 3RD PARTY INTENT WORKS
A complete explanation of what 3rd party intent data is, how it's collected, and exactly how Younion uses it to find companies ready to buy — before they contact you.
🌐

What Is 3rd Party Intent?

External platforms like Bombora, G2 and ZoomInfo track browsing behaviour across thousands of B2B websites and review platforms. When employees at a company repeatedly research topics like "event management agency" or "brand activation", these platforms record it as a buying signal — even before they visit younion.live.

📡

How Signals Are Collected

Bombora runs a co-op of 5,000+ B2B publisher websites. When someone from Tata Consumer reads 3+ articles about "experiential marketing" in 2 weeks, Bombora records a topic surge for that company domain. G2 tracks when companies compare you vs competitors. Signals are anonymised at person level but identifiable at company (domain) level.

⚡

Why It Works for Younion

80% of the B2B buying journey happens before a buyer ever contacts a vendor. By the time someone fills your contact form, they've already shortlisted vendors. 3rd party intent lets Younion's sales team enter the journey early — when prospects are still researching — giving a 3–4 week head start on competitors.

Each Source Explained
🌊Bombora

Tracks 5,000+ B2B websites. You upload your "Surge Topics" (e.g., "experiential marketing") and get weekly alerts when any company in your Target Account List spikes on those topics. Best for early-stage research signals.

Setup: Upload TAL → Define topics → Get weekly CSV → Push to CRM
⭐G2 / Capterra

Shows which companies viewed Younion's profile, compared you to competitors, or read your reviews. This is the highest-quality signal because the buyer was actively evaluating vendors — not just browsing content.

Setup: Claim G2 profile → Enable intent add-on → Connect to HubSpot → Done
🚀Apollo.io

B2B contact database with intent flags. Search decision-makers (CMOs, Marketing Heads) at ICP companies, filter by "recently changed job" or "hiring for marketing" signals, and export to CRM in one click. Free tier is excellent to start.

Setup: Sign up free → Set ICP filters → Export 100 contacts/month free
🔭ZoomInfo

Enterprise B2B intelligence with intent signals tracked across a large publisher network, overlaid with company firmographic data (revenue, headcount, tech stack). Run a 14-day free trial before committing.

Setup: Request trial → Upload TAL → Define intent topics → Weekly report
💼LinkedIn Intent

Company page analytics shows who viewed your page. Sales Navigator shows which decision-makers viewed your team profiles. Matched Audiences let you retarget your TAL. One of the richest signals for India B2B.

Setup: Admin access → Company Page Analytics → Sales Nav profile view tracking
🏆Clutch / IndiaMart

India-specific B2B directories. Companies searching "event management agency Bangalore" on these platforms are actively shortlisting vendors — highly commercial, high-intent signal. Free listing available immediately.

Setup: List on Clutch.co free → Add case studies → Monitor enquiries daily

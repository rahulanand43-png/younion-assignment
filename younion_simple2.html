How to Connect All Intent Sources

Simple guide for Younion · 8 weeks · Start free today

🌊 Bombora ⭐ G2 🚀 Apollo 🔭 ZoomInfo 💼 LinkedIn 🏆 Clutch → 📊 Google Sheet → 🏠 HubSpot CRM → 📞 Sales Acts
Week 1 Build the Hub Free · 3–4 hrs
1
Create HubSpot free account
Go to hubspot.com → sign up free. No credit card needed.
Free forever
2
Add 4 custom fields to HubSpot companies
Settings → Properties → Company → Create property. Add: Intent Score (number), Intent Level (dropdown: High/Medium/Low), Intent Source (text), Last Signal Date (date).
20 minutes
3
Create "Intent Master" Google Sheet
Make a sheet with 7 tabs: MASTER, BOMBORA, G2, APOLLO, ZOOMINFO, LINKEDIN, CLUTCH. Every Monday you paste each source's export into its tab. MASTER calculates the scores.
Freesheets.google.com
4
Import your Target Account List into HubSpot
Export your 200–500 target companies as CSV → HubSpot → Import → Companies. Needs: Company Name, Domain, Industry.
Week 2 Connect Free Sources G2 · Apollo · Clutch · LinkedIn
💡 These 4 sources are completely free. Connect them before spending any money.
5
Claim Younion's G2 profile → connect to HubSpot
Go to g2.com → search Younion → Claim → fill in services + 3 case studies. Then: G2 admin → Integrations → HubSpot → Connect. Done. Every profile view auto-updates HubSpot.
Free · Native integration · No code
6
Sign up Apollo.io → connect to HubSpot
Go to apollo.io → sign up free → Settings → Integrations → HubSpot → Connect. Set ICP filters: CMO / Marketing Head / Brand Manager at FMCG, BFSI, Pharma companies in India. Export 100 contacts/month free.
100 exports/month free · Native integration
7
List on Clutch.co → auto-push enquiries to HubSpot
Go to clutch.co → List Younion → add services + 5 portfolio projects. Then go to zapier.com → create a Zap: Clutch enquiry email → HubSpot new contact. Every enquiry auto-creates a CRM record.
Clutch free · Zapier free tier
8
LinkedIn: export analytics weekly → paste into Sheet
Every Monday: LinkedIn Company Page → Analytics → Visitor Analytics → Export XLS → paste companies from your TAL into the LINKEDIN tab. Takes 5 minutes.
Free · 5 min/week
Week 3–4 Add Paid Sources (After Validating) ZoomInfo · Bombora
⚠️ Only do this after your free sources are working and giving signals. Don't spend money before you've proven the process.
9
Start ZoomInfo 14-day free trial
Go to zoominfo.com → request trial → upload your TAL → set intent topics ("B2B Event Management", "Experiential Marketing", "Brand Activation") → connect to HubSpot natively.
14-day free trial
10
Add Bombora via HubSpot Marketplace
HubSpot → Marketplace → search "Bombora" → Install → configure surge topics. Bombora pushes weekly data directly into your HubSpot company records automatically. No CSV needed.
~₹42,000/month · Add only after validating ZoomInfo
Week 5–6 Automate with Zapier 3 automations · Zero manual work after

Zapier free

Zapier free
13
Zap 3 — High Intent → task + email sequence
Trigger: HubSpot company Intent Score reaches 60 → Action: Create SDR task "Call [Company] today" → Action: Enroll in High Intent email sequence.
Zapier free
